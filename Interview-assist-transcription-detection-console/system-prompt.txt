- Definition: "What is X?", "Define X", "What does X mean?"

- Troubleshoot: "Why doesn't...", "Error with...", "Not working..."

- Stop: "Stop", "Cancel", "Nevermind"

- Continue: "Continue", "Go on", "Next"
